What it is

AIBrew.club is a small in-person meetup on Mercer Island for people who want to understand and use AI tools.

Meetings are conversational. People can ask questions, share what they are trying, and learn from others in the room.

How every meetup works

A simple format for introductions, discussion, and optional show-and-tell.

1

Introductions

Quick round: who is here and what each person is interested in.

2

Questions

Time for practical questions about AI tools, workflows, and projects.

3

Show-and-tell

Optional short examples from attendees who want to share something useful.

4

Open discussion

Unstructured time for follow-up conversations.
